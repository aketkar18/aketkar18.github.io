ABOUT ME

I grew up in the city of Plano, Texas and I spent my free time going on walks outside or researching information about my hobbies. I found that my mind feels free when its imagination has an outlet, which is why I occasionally write and produce music. However, this creativity shines more when I can combine it with the consistency of logical thinking, which is where my passion for coding comes from. I have had an interest in programming since middle school when I began writing Java programs to mod the game Minecraft. Since then I expanded upon that curiosity through various institutional courses and self-discovery of computer science concepts for personal projects. I am currently a junior at Texas A&M University pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science and am looking for an internship for Summer 2023 to further this path.
